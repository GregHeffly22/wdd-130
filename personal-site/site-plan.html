Overview

Purpose

Are you a shoe connoisseur? Do you like Van's shoes more than any other? Then this is the site for you. Here at Only Vans we talk about the different styles of vans, and rate them. You don't need to visit the vans store to get an accurate view of their shoes. I have been wearing shoes all my life, so I know shoes. If the van exists, I probably will review it. The choice about which pair of shoes to make has never been so easy!

Audience

My audience is anyone who wears shoes, so basically everyone! Anyone who can read, and loves shoes will get a benefit. The age I am targeting, however, is going to be kids and young adults from about 12-30. Vans has always been a thing for younger people,and so we will cater to them. This would help advertisers find a way to advertise to my visitor base.
